About Me.


I am a software developer with 15 years of experience in enterprise service desk management, currently working as a Team Lead. Throughout my career, I have gained extensive experience in managing complex IT infrastructure and providing top-notch customer support.


While working in the service desk field, I discovered my passion for coding and decided to make a career switch to become a software developer. I have since built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, Angular, and Javascript. I am constantly learning and improving my skills to create intuitive and user- friendly applications.


My goal as a software developer is to leverage my years of experience in customer support to create applications that not only meet but exceed user expectations. I am excited to embark on this new journey and look forward to collaborating with others to create innovative solutions. Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio!
